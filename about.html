Dearest reader,

Re-Xon is a website designed to help relax the mind through creative media, such as photography and art. Our aim is to give students a space to share their thought provocative artwork and showcase what makes their world colourful or happy. We understand a student does not have much time on their hands, and at moments forget or neglegt their previous passions under the power of study influences. Through Re-Xon, you will be able to share your adventures with like-minded individuals who want to showcase their passions in the world through these artistic modes. We want to inspire you to inspire others, to exhibit the beauty throughout the world and study institutions, we want minds to relax and digest the gaze of the world through a different eye, a different perspected, their world. We want to give you an interesting method of relaxing, through art, through both the gaze and through contribution.
